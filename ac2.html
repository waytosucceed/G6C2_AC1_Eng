Synonyms

Theme of the Poem -

What would have been the scenario if there had been no language? How would we
communicate with each other?

Words are what help us to express our thoughts and ideas. The poet says that if you have any
thought or idea but you do not know how to say it, just pick up the words that will express that
thought or idea in the best way possible.

Overview of Synonyms:

Synonyms are words that have the exact meaning or nearly the exact meaning. They help
us in increasing our vocabulary and in avoiding the repetition of the same word. Synonyms
help us in adding variety to the language therefore enhancing our writing skills.
